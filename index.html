Crystals Collector Game

You have been given a random target score to obtain.

Each of the four crystals have been assigned a random point value, this value will change with each game reset and will not be displayed to you. Whenever a crystal is clicked its value will be added to your total score.

Your goal is to click on crystals in such a way that your total score matches the target score. If your total score goes the over target score the game is over and you have lost.

Target Score:

100

Current Score:

50
